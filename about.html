Gameplay

Click 'New Game' to begin the game.

Use the left mouse button (LMB) to click on a cell on the grid. If you hit a bomb, you lose. Use the right mouse button (RMB) to click and mark the cell on the grid with a flag where you think a bomb is. Use the middle mouse button (MMB) click over grid to pause the game.

The numbers on the board represent how many bombs are adjacent to a cell. The bombs could be above, below, right, left or diagonal to the square. For example, '1' indicates that there's only one mine around, while '8' indicates that the cell is surounded by mines.

Mark all the mines or expose all empty cells to win the game.
